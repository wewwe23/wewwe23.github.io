线性规划的数学模型

\[ max(min)Z=c_{1}x_{1}+c_{2}x_{2}+...+c_{j}x_{j}+...+c_{n}x_{n} \]

\[ \begin{equation} s.t.\left\{ \begin{aligned} a_{11}x_{1}+a_{12}x_{2}+...+a_{1n}x_{n} & \leq(=,\geq) & b_{1}\\ a_{21}x_{1}+a_{22}x_{2}+...+a_{2n}x_{n} & \leq(=,\geq) & b_{2}\\ ... ... ... ...\\ a_{i1}x_{1}+a_{i2}x_{2}+...+a_{in}x_{n} & \leq(=,\geq) & b_{i}\\ ... ... ... ...\\ a_{m1}x_{1}+a_{m2}x_{2}+...+a_{mn}x_{n} & \leq(=,\geq) & b_{m}\\ x_{1},x_{2},...,x_{n}\geq0 \end{aligned} \right. \end{equation} \]

\[ c_{j}:价值系数 \]

\[ a_{ij}:工艺或技术系数 \]

\[ b_{i}:资源限制 \]

\[ x_1,x_2,...,x_n\geq0:决策变量约束 \]
